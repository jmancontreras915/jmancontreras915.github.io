First note in a quieter room.

There is a kind of writing space that does not ask for much. A title, a date, a few paragraphs, and enough quiet to let the first sentence be imperfect.

I wanted this blog to start there. Not as a product launch or a polished declaration, but as a shelf for thoughts that would otherwise stay scattered.

The hope is simple: return often, write plainly, and leave enough evidence of the inner weather to recognize the season later.
